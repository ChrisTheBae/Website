The Archon Staff

First off welcome to team, this is a guide on how to act as a staff member and the correct punishments to issue. Some of these may change from time to time so please check back frequently.

It is your job to represent this server, both on and off the server. You are the face of TheArchon, and you are expected to act accordingly. This document will contain all necessary information to get you started, learn the ropes of how to be a staff member, and what your goals should be to progress and become a successful staff member on this server.
